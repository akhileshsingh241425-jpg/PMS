# Requirements Document

## Introduction

Enhancement of the INFOCUS-IT PMS v2 Client Portal to provide external clients with a comprehensive self-service experience. The current portal allows clients to view project status, download approved documents, and write notes. This enhancement adds meeting scheduling, document revision requests, document uploads from client side, query management on audit findings, remediation tracking, invoice/payment visibility, and follow-up audit scheduling — covering everything a client of a cybersecurity audit firm needs from their perspective.

## Glossary

- **Client_Portal**: The external-facing web application where authenticated client users access their project information, documents, meetings, and communications with INFOCUS-IT
- **Client_User**: An external user (employee of the client organization) who has login credentials for the Client Portal, linked to an Account
- **Account**: The client organization's master record in the PMS system
- **Project**: An audit engagement (VAPT, IS Audit, ISMS, RBI Audit, Compliance) being delivered to the client
- **Meeting_Request**: A request submitted by a client to schedule a meeting with the INFOCUS-IT audit team
- **Document_Revision_Request**: A formal request from the client to revise or update a shared document, with specific comments on what needs changing
- **Client_Upload**: A document uploaded by the client (policies, network diagrams, evidence) required by the audit team
- **Finding_Query**: A question or clarification request raised by the client on a specific audit finding in a report
- **Remediation_Item**: A specific action item from audit findings that the client must address, with status tracking
- **Invoice**: A billing record generated by INFOCUS-IT for services rendered to the client
- **Audit_Team**: The INFOCUS-IT internal staff (Project Manager, Consultants) assigned to the client's project
- **Notification**: An alert sent to the client when relevant events occur (document shared, meeting confirmed, query responded)

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Meeting Scheduling

**User Story:** As a client, I want to request and schedule meetings with the audit team, so that I can discuss project progress, findings, or any concerns directly.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a client submits a meeting request with a preferred date, time, and agenda, THE Client_Portal SHALL create a Meeting_Request with status "Requested" and notify the Audit_Team
2. WHEN the Audit_Team confirms a Meeting_Request, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the Meeting_Request status to "Confirmed" and notify the Client_User
3. WHEN the Audit_Team proposes an alternative time for a Meeting_Request, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the Meeting_Request with the proposed time and status "Rescheduled" and notify the Client_User
4. WHEN a Client_User views the meetings section, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all Meeting_Requests for the Client_User's Account with their current status, sorted by date descending
5. WHEN a Client_User or Audit_Team cancels a confirmed meeting, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the Meeting_Request status to "Cancelled" and notify the other party
6. IF a Client_User submits a Meeting_Request without a preferred date or agenda, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL reject the request and display a validation error indicating the missing fields

### Requirement 2: Document Revision Requests

**User Story:** As a client, I want to request changes or revisions to shared audit documents, so that I can ensure reports accurately reflect our environment and findings.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User submits a Document_Revision_Request on an approved document with specific comments, THE Client_Portal SHALL create the request with status "Submitted" and notify the Audit_Team
2. WHEN the Audit_Team acknowledges a Document_Revision_Request, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the request status to "Under Review"
3. WHEN the Audit_Team resolves a Document_Revision_Request by uploading a revised document, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the request status to "Resolved" and notify the Client_User
4. WHEN a Client_User views revision requests, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all Document_Revision_Requests for the Client_User's Account with status and linked document name
5. IF a Client_User submits a Document_Revision_Request without specifying comments describing the requested change, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL reject the request and display a validation error

### Requirement 3: Client Document Upload

**User Story:** As a client, I want to upload documents (policies, network diagrams, compliance evidence) to the audit team, so that auditors have the information they need without relying on email exchanges.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User uploads a document to a project with a category and description, THE Client_Portal SHALL store the Client_Upload and notify the Audit_Team
2. THE Client_Portal SHALL restrict Client_Upload file types to PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PNG, JPG, and ZIP formats
3. THE Client_Portal SHALL restrict each Client_Upload file size to a maximum of 25 megabytes
4. WHEN a Client_User views uploaded documents, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all Client_Uploads for the selected project with file name, category, upload date, and acknowledgement status
5. WHEN the Audit_Team acknowledges receipt of a Client_Upload, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the upload status to "Acknowledged" and notify the Client_User
6. IF a Client_User uploads a file with an unsupported format, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL reject the upload and display an error indicating the allowed file types

### Requirement 4: Finding Queries

**User Story:** As a client, I want to raise queries on specific audit findings, so that I can get clarifications and understand the implications of reported vulnerabilities or non-conformities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User submits a Finding_Query linked to a specific document with a subject and question text, THE Client_Portal SHALL create the query with status "Open" and notify the Audit_Team
2. WHEN the Audit_Team responds to a Finding_Query, THE Client_Portal SHALL save the response, update the query status to "Answered", and notify the Client_User
3. WHEN a Client_User views finding queries, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all Finding_Queries for the Client_User's Account grouped by project, showing status and response if available
4. WHEN a Client_User submits a follow-up on an answered Finding_Query, THE Client_Portal SHALL reopen the query with status "Reopened" and notify the Audit_Team
5. IF a Client_User submits a Finding_Query without a subject or question text, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL reject the query and display a validation error indicating the missing fields

### Requirement 5: Remediation Tracking

**User Story:** As a client, I want to track the remediation status of audit findings, so that I can monitor progress on fixing identified vulnerabilities and ensure compliance deadlines are met.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN the Audit_Team publishes remediation items for a project, THE Client_Portal SHALL display each Remediation_Item with its title, severity, description, due date, and current status
2. WHEN a Client_User updates the status of a Remediation_Item to "In Progress" or "Completed" with evidence or comments, THE Client_Portal SHALL save the update and notify the Audit_Team
3. WHEN the Audit_Team verifies a completed Remediation_Item, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the status to "Verified" and notify the Client_User
4. WHEN a Client_User views the remediation dashboard, THE Client_Portal SHALL display a summary showing total items, items by severity, items by status, and percentage of completion
5. WHILE a Remediation_Item has a due date within 7 days and status is not "Completed" or "Verified", THE Client_Portal SHALL display the item with a visual warning indicator

### Requirement 6: Invoice and Payment Visibility

**User Story:** As a client, I want to view invoices and payment status for my projects, so that I can track billing, process payments on time, and maintain financial records.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User navigates to the invoices section, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all Invoices for the Client_User's Account with invoice number, date, amount, due date, and payment status
2. WHEN an Invoice payment status changes, THE Client_Portal SHALL reflect the updated status (Pending, Partial Payment, Paid) and notify the Client_User
3. THE Client_Portal SHALL allow Client_Users to download Invoice documents in PDF format
4. WHEN a Client_User views invoice details, THE Client_Portal SHALL display line items, tax breakdown, total amount, payment history, and linked project name
5. IF no invoices exist for the Client_User's Account, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL display an informative empty state message

### Requirement 7: Follow-up Audit Scheduling

**User Story:** As a client, I want to request follow-up or renewal audits, so that I can maintain continuous compliance and schedule recurring assessments without manual coordination.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User submits a follow-up audit request specifying the service type, preferred start period, and scope description, THE Client_Portal SHALL create the request with status "Submitted" and notify the Audit_Team
2. WHEN the Audit_Team reviews and accepts a follow-up audit request, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the request status to "Accepted" and notify the Client_User with estimated timeline
3. WHEN the Audit_Team declines a follow-up audit request, THE Client_Portal SHALL update the status to "Declined" with a reason and notify the Client_User
4. WHEN a Client_User views follow-up audit requests, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all requests for the Client_User's Account with status, service type, and submitted date
5. IF a Client_User submits a follow-up audit request without specifying service type or scope description, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL reject the request and display a validation error

### Requirement 8: Client Notifications

**User Story:** As a client, I want to receive notifications about important events (new documents shared, meeting confirmations, query responses), so that I stay informed without repeatedly checking the portal.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN an event relevant to a Client_User occurs (document shared, meeting confirmed, query answered, invoice raised, remediation item published), THE Client_Portal SHALL create a Notification for the Client_User
2. WHEN a Client_User views the notifications section, THE Client_Portal SHALL display all Notifications sorted by date descending with read/unread status
3. WHEN a Client_User clicks on a Notification, THE Client_Portal SHALL mark the Notification as read and navigate to the relevant section
4. THE Client_Portal SHALL display an unread notification count badge in the portal header
5. WHEN a Client_User marks all notifications as read, THE Client_Portal SHALL update all unread Notifications to read status

### Requirement 9: Client Profile Management

**User Story:** As a client, I want to manage my profile information and change my password, so that I can keep my contact details current and maintain account security.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User updates their name or phone number, THE Client_Portal SHALL save the changes and display a confirmation message
2. WHEN a Client_User changes their password by providing the current password and a new password, THE Client_Portal SHALL validate the current password, update to the new password, and display a confirmation message
3. IF a Client_User provides an incorrect current password during password change, THEN THE Client_Portal SHALL reject the change and display an error message
4. THE Client_Portal SHALL enforce a minimum password length of 8 characters for new passwords
5. WHEN a Client_User views their profile, THE Client_Portal SHALL display the user's name, email (read-only), phone, and company name

### Requirement 10: Portal Dashboard Overview

**User Story:** As a client, I want to see a consolidated dashboard when I log in, so that I can quickly understand the current state of all my engagements at a glance.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a Client_User logs into the Client_Portal, THE Client_Portal SHALL display a dashboard showing active project count, pending remediation items count, unread notifications count, and upcoming meetings count
2. WHEN a Client_User views the dashboard, THE Client_Portal SHALL display the three most recent Notifications as a quick-view list
3. WHEN a Client_User views the dashboard, THE Client_Portal SHALL display active projects with their current stage and progress summary
4. WHEN a Client_User clicks on any dashboard summary card, THE Client_Portal SHALL navigate to the corresponding detailed section
